About MonumentID

Discovering the world's heritage, one monument at a time, powered by Artificial Intelligence.

Our Mission

At MonumentID, our mission is to leverage cutting-edge AI to make history and culture more accessible. We aim to provide a seamless experience for users to identify historical monuments, learn about their significance, and foster a deeper appreciation for global heritage.

Whether you're a student, traveler, researcher, or simply curious, MonumentID is your digital guide to the architectural wonders of the past.

How It Works

1. Upload Image

Simply upload a clear image of the monument you want to identify.

2. AI Analysis

Our advanced AI model analyzes the image, recognizing key architectural features.

3. Get Results

Receive the monument's name, history, location, and architectural details instantly.

Technology Powering MonumentID

Core AI Model

  • Deep Learning Model: ResNet152V2 (Fine-tuned)
  • Framework: TensorFlow / Keras
  • Model Language: Python

Frontend & Utilities

  • Markup & Styling: HTML5, CSS
  • Interactivity: JavaScript
  • Web Framework: Django
  • UI Components: Font Awesome Icons
